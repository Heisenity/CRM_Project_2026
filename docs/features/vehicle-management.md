# Vehicle Management

**Feature name:** Vehicle Management  
**Route:** `/vehicles`

## What this feature does
Vehicle Management helps you maintain company vehicle records and manage fuel bill actions.

You can use this page to:
- Add new vehicles.
- Edit or remove existing vehicles.
- Search and filter vehicle records.
- Review fuel bill submissions.
- Approve or reject pending fuel bills.

## When users should use it
Use this page when you need to:
- Register a new vehicle in the system.
- Update vehicle details.
- Track available and assigned vehicles.
- Process petrol/fuel bill requests.

## Step-by-step instructions
1. Open the app, then click **Task Management** in the left sidebar. In the top-right area, click **Vehicle** to open **Vehicle Management**.
2. Review the current tab (vehicles or bills) based on your task.
3. To add a vehicle:
   - Click **Add New Vehicle**.
   - Enter required details (vehicle number, type, and other fields).
   - Save the record.
4. Use search and filters to find a vehicle quickly.
5. Open row actions to edit or delete a vehicle when needed.
6. Switch to fuel/petrol bill records to review submitted bills.
7. Open bill details and verify amount, date, and related vehicle information.
8. Approve or reject the bill based on review.
9. Confirm the updated status is visible in the list.

## Expected result
Vehicle and fuel bill records remain accurate, updated, and easy to track.

Typical outcomes:
- New vehicles appear in the list after creation.
- Updated vehicle details are reflected correctly.
- Fuel bills move to approved/rejected status after review.

## Common mistakes or errors
- Vehicle not visible: Check active tab and clear filters.
- Cannot save vehicle: Ensure required fields are completed.
- Duplicate vehicle number: Use a unique vehicle number.
- Bill not found: Verify date/filter settings and refresh the page.
- Wrong bill status selected: Reopen bill details and update carefully.
