# HR Center

**Feature name:** HR Center  
**Route:** `/hr-center`

## What this feature does
HR Center is the main workspace for HR-related tasks.

You can use this page to:
- Review and manage employee leave requests.
- Upload and manage employee documents.
- Open Payslip Management for salary slip tasks.
- Re-enable clock-in access when an employee has attendance issues.

## When users should use it
Use this page when you need to:
- Approve or reject leave applications.
- Maintain employee documents in one place.
- Handle payroll document workflow.
- Support employees with attendance-related access issues.

## Step-by-step instructions
1. Open the app and go to **HR Center** from the left sidebar.
2. Review the top actions and choose what you need.
3. In **Leave Management**, open pending leave requests and take action.
4. In **Document Management**, upload or update employee documents.
5. If needed, click **Payslip Management** to open payroll slip tasks.
6. If an employee cannot clock in, use **Re-enable Clock-In** and complete the required action.
7. Confirm your updates are saved and visible in the relevant section.

## Expected result
HR tasks are handled from one page, and employee records are kept updated.

Typical outcomes:
- Leave requests move from pending to approved/rejected.
- Employee documents are uploaded and available.
- Payslip-related work is opened quickly from HR Center.
- Attendance access issues are resolved faster.

## Common mistakes or errors
- Access denied: Your account may not have HR Center permission.
- Leave action not saved: Ensure required fields or comments are provided.
- Document upload failed: Check file format/size and retry.
- Payslip page not opening: Verify HR access and try again.
- Clock-in still not working after reset: Ask the employee to sign out and sign in again.
