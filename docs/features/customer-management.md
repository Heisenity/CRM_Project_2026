# Customer Management

**Feature name:** Customer Management  
**Route:** `/customers`

## What this feature does
Customer Management helps you maintain complete customer records in one place.

You can use this page to:
- Add new customer records.
- Search and filter existing customers.
- View complete customer details.
- Edit customer information.
- Delete customer records when no longer needed.

## When users should use it
Use this page when you need to:
- Register a new customer.
- Update customer phone, email, or address.
- Verify customer details before support or project work.
- Review customer record history.

## Step-by-step instructions
1. Open the app and go to **Customer Management** from the left sidebar.
2. To create a customer, click **Add Customer**.
3. Fill required customer details (name, phone, and other fields as needed).
4. Save the customer record.
5. Use the search box to find customers by customer ID, name, or phone.
6. Open row actions for a customer:
   - **View** to check full customer details.
   - **Edit** to update customer information.
   - **Delete** to remove a customer record.
7. Save any edits and confirm changes appear in the list.

## Expected result
Customer records are accurate, searchable, and ready for day-to-day business use.

Typical outcomes:
- New customers are added with unique customer IDs.
- Updated contact details are visible immediately.
- Teams can quickly access correct customer information.

## Common mistakes or errors
- Customer not saved: Complete all required fields before submitting.
- Duplicate entry: Search existing records before adding a new customer.
- Customer not found: Clear filters and search by ID, name, or phone.
- Wrong record edited: Verify customer name and ID before saving.
- Delete done by mistake: Confirm details carefully before deleting.
