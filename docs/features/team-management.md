# Team Management

**Feature name:** Team Management  
**Route:** `/teams`

## What this feature does
Team Management helps you organize employees into working teams.

You can use this page to:
- Create new teams.
- Edit existing team details.
- Delete teams that are no longer needed.
- Keep team structure clear for daily operations.

## When users should use it
Use this page when you need to:
- Set up a new team.
- Rename or update a team.
- Maintain a clean team list.
- Align staff structure with current work assignments.

## Step-by-step instructions
1. Open the app, then go to **Team Management** in the left sidebar..
2. Review the current team list.
3. To create a team, click **Create Team**.
4. Enter team details and save.
5. To update a team, click **Edit** on that team and save changes.
6. To remove a team, click **Delete** and confirm.
7. Refresh the page to confirm the latest team list.

## Expected result
Teams are clearly structured, up to date, and easy to manage.

Typical outcomes:
- New teams appear in the list after creation.
- Team name/details updates are visible immediately.
- Unused teams are removed from active records.

## Common mistakes or errors
- Team not visible after create: Refresh the page and check again.
- Save failed: Ensure required team fields are filled.
- Wrong team edited: Verify team name before saving changes.
- Delete blocked: Remove dependencies (if any) and retry.
- Team deleted by mistake: Recreate the team with correct details.
