# Dashboard

**Feature name:** Dashboard  
**Route:** `/dashboard`

## What this feature does
Dashboard is your main control screen. It shows live business updates in one place so you can quickly see what needs attention.

Main areas include:
- Total Employees
- Stock Items
- Active Tasks
- Present Today (attendance)
- Pending Leave Applications
- Recent Tickets
- Vehicle Overview
- Customer Support alert banner (when requests are pending)

## When users should use it
Use Dashboard:
- At the start of the day to check current status.
- During the day to track new issues and requests.
- Before end of day to confirm pending items are handled.

## Step-by-step instructions
1. Open the app. In the left sidebar, click **Dashboard**.
2. Read the top number cards:
   - **Total Employees**: total registered staff.
   - **Stock Items**: total items in inventory.
   - **Active Tasks**: currently tracked tasks.
   - **Present Today**: how many employees are marked present today.
3. Check the orange support banner:
   - If visible, click **View Requests** to open customer support requests.
4. Review the activity cards:
   - **Pending Leave Applications**: leave requests waiting for review.
   - **Recent Tickets**: latest support tickets and their priority.
   - **Vehicle Overview**: total vehicles and available vehicles.
5. Click **View All** on any card to open that full page and take action.
6. If numbers look old, refresh the page.

## Expected result
You can understand business status in under a minute and move directly to the right page for action.

Typical outcomes:
- You spot pending leave requests and open HR Center.
- You spot high-priority tickets and open Ticket Management.
- You see pending customer support requests and assign them quickly.

## Common mistakes or errors
- Access denied or redirect: Your account may not have Dashboard permission.
- Numbers look incorrect: Refresh once and check again after a few seconds.
- No recent tickets shown: There may be no new tickets yet.
- No leave applications shown: There may be no pending leave requests.
