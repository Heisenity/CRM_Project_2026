# Meetings

**Feature name:** Meetings  
**Route:** `/meetings`

## What this feature does
Meetings helps you plan, track, and manage all meetings from one page.

You can use this page to:
- Create new meetings.
- View today’s schedule.
- Track upcoming meetings.
- Search and filter meetings.
- Edit or delete meeting records.

## When users should use it
Use this page when you need to:
- Schedule a new internal or client meeting.
- Check what meetings are happening today.
- Review upcoming meetings for planning.
- Update meeting details after schedule changes.

## Step-by-step instructions
1. Open the app and go to **Meetings** from the sidebar.
2. Click **New Meeting**.
3. Fill in meeting details (title, date/time, meeting type, priority, attendees, and notes if needed).
4. Save the meeting.
5. Use tabs to view meetings in different ways:
   - **Overview & All Meetings**: quick view of all items.
   - **Today’s Schedule**: meetings for today only.
   - **Upcoming**: future scheduled meetings.
   - **Detailed View**: full list with more information.
6. Use search and filters (status/type) to find specific meetings.
7. Open a meeting to view full details.
8. Use **Edit** when details change, or **Delete** when a meeting should be removed.

## Expected result
Meetings are well organized, easy to find, and kept up to date.

Typical outcomes:
- New meetings appear in the correct tab after saving.
- Today’s schedule is clear for daily planning.
- Updated meeting details are visible immediately after edit.

## Common mistakes or errors
- Meeting not visible: Check active tab, date, and filters.
- Wrong meeting time: Edit the meeting and save corrected date/time.
- Duplicate meetings: Search by title/date before creating a new one.
- Cannot edit/delete: Confirm you have required permission.
- Missing attendees: Reopen the meeting and update attendee list.
